neokingdom DAO

Mission Statement

Neokingdoms are constitutional DAOcracies. They serve a collective of humans with a common goal. Fairness, decision making, and rewarding of those who create value are technology based and happen in the virtual realm. The purpose is to create a better existence for everyone on earth.

Definition

Neokingdoms are EVM based, legally compliant DAOs which rely on a legal model crafted by Oblicity, an Estonian law firm using the favorable Estonian legal system.

ODOO, an open source ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning System) is used as the digital backbone to be able to run and administer a full-fledged organization or brick-and-mortar businesses for real-world uses cases.

Users vote and interact with the DAO using a progressive web app.

The core principle is that contributors of the DAO can invest their personal time or money to receive shares of the organization. Those shares represent ownership, voting and dividend rights.

How

We are building neokingdom DAO for real world use cases. To find the right balance between idealism and pragmatism, we decided to test the system ourselves while we are developing it—some people call this approach dogfooding, some others call it having skin in the game.

That's why on July 27th, 2022 we transformed our company, teledisko GmbH into the first neokingdom. This means that the decisional process of the company happens on–chain, contributors are rewarded Teledisko Tokens, and many other things that you can find in our legal documentation.

We are using the system we built to test our assumptions, and we are actively evolving the legal and technical framework to adapt it to the challenges we are facing. Once we are ready we will make our framework, neokingdom, available to everyone.

We recently took part in the EVMOS Momentum Hackathon, and neokingdom DAO has been awarded the second prize in the web3 track.
